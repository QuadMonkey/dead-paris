DEAD PARIS
Le Meurice Hotel • 228 Rue de Rivoli • Paris, 1st Arr.

You open your eyes.

The ceiling is ornate — gold leaf, baroque plasterwork, a crystal chandelier hanging at an angle that isn’t right. The bed beneath you is absurdly soft. Egyptian cotton. Thread count in the thousands.

You’re in Room 302 of Le Meurice, one of the most expensive hotels in Paris.

You don’t remember how long you’ve been out. The last thing you remember is the news. The outbreak. The airports closing. Emergency broadcasts in French you couldn’t fully understand, but the tone was universal. Run. Hide. Pray.

Something hits the window. Then again. You look out across the Tuileries Garden and see them — dozens of figures stumbling between the chestnut trees. The fountains are red.

Paris has fallen. And you are alone.

How to Play

Paris is overrun. You woke up in Le Meurice hotel with no memory of how you got here. Scavenge supplies, fight the undead, and find a way out — or survive long enough for rescue.

Survive 30 days or escape the city via one of four routes.

Staying Alive

♥ Health
If your health drops to 0, you die. Use medicine or rest to heal.
★ Hunger
Drains over time. Eat food to restore it. You will start to take damage below 20.
◌ Thirst
Drains faster than hunger. Drink water to restore it. You will start to take damage below 30.

☾ Night (21:00–06:00) is far more dangerous. Zombies are more active and encounters are deadlier.

Escape Routes

⛺ Seine Boat Repair a motorboat at Port de Solferino
✈ CDG Airport Drive north with fuel and a working radio
⛫ Catacombs Navigate underground tunnels south out of the city
☊ Helicopter Signal military extraction from the hotel rooftop

Tips

  • Explore the hotel thoroughly before venturing outside.
  • The crowbar in the wine cellar opens the hotel entrance.
  • Talk to every survivor — they hold escape route clues.
  • Save often. Death is permanent.
